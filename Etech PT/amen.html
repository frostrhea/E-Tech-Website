Autobiography of Amen L. Gorondaya

October 18, 2020

My name is Amen L Gorondaya but you can call me men for short. I am 18 years old and I was born 31st of july in 2002. My mother is Mosripha L Gorondaya and my father is Mohaymen L Gorondaya they are both enterprenuer and we're currently living in Cagayan De Oro City. We are six(6) siblings and I am the youngest I have 4 sisters and 1 brother. 4 of my siblings already have professions in the fields of medicine and accounting.
Now for you to know me better I will share the things I like and I don't like. I like playing sports and video games also I love to watch movies and listen to musics. In addition I am good at cooking I always experiment and make my own recipe.
The things I don't like are eating vegetables because when I was a kid I really found vegetable as not delicious. Another thing I don't like is when my friends making fun of me by running in the street when there is a dog around. I really don't like dogs because I have a trauma to it because when I was a kid I got bitten by a dog and got injections for anti-rabies, thats why I'm scared.
Now I will share my characteristic to you for you to be able to know me better. I'm a type of person who is really shy and not friendly but when you start talking to me I will entertain you. Another is that I easily get emotional when I see sad movies and when I see the unprivileged people suffers in life. The characteristic of mine that I really like is that I am always willing to help anyone as much as I can because for me helping is caring.
Now lets talk about my goals in life. The 1st goal that I really want to achieve is to graduate and have a work for me to be able to repay my parents. 2nd is I want to travel the world with my family and friends. 3rd is to buy the things that I can't buy now but the most important goal that I really want to achieve is to help my fellow muslims whose in need.
Now you know me better, I hope you enjoy reading my autobiography.
